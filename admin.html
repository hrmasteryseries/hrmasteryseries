3 · Publish Your Changes

When you have finished editing, click the button below. It downloads one file: data.js. Then:

  1. Go to your website's GitHub page and open the data folder.
  2. Click Add file → Upload files, drop in the downloaded data.js, and click Commit changes.
  3. Wait 1–2 minutes. Your live website updates automatically.

New cover images or PDF resources? Upload them the same way into the covers or resources folder first. Full click-by-click instructions are in your Setup Guide.
